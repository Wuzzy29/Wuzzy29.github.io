Tumaini Senior Secondary School

Tumaini Senior Secondary School is a privately school founded by the board members of the famous Tumaini Junior School. Tumaini Senior Secondary School is located at Makuyuni, Arusha. The school admits both boys and girls for Ordinary Level and Advanced Level by June of 2020. Tumaini Senior Secondary School was registered by the year 2016 with the examination number: S.5493

Due to the outstanding performance of students, It is the first school to be inscribed in Monduli. Tumaini Senior Secondary School was recognized as a part of Tumaini Junior Primary School in Karatu.
